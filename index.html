Welcome to my website!

I'm still working things out here, as you can see. I feel the need to create an about page and a landing page, though I don't yet know what might be interesting to share about myself, or what sort of things ought to go on a landing page, so for now I hope this will suffice.

The site is intentionally minimal, as I have a tendency to overcomplicate things, and I want to focus on publishing insightful content rather than obsess over layouts and design. I am also trying to put my ideas out there and iterate quickly so for the first version of the site I have decided to only work on a few things:

  • This short introduction explaining my thought process
  • A contact form so that anyone reading this can make suggestions or just let me know what if anything you found valuable (I would really appreciate it).
  • A list of posts which I'm allowing to be less so complete articles and more short thoughts to see what people are interested in.

I plan to update this site fairly often as I develop my ideas about what I want to do with it, and I plan to document the though process behind many of these changes. In essence, this site represents a record of my thoughts, which is as much for my only clarity of mind than anything else.

Feel free to check back later to see if I've made any changes or added some new content. If you have manage to find this site by some miraculous then drop me a line using the contact form: introduce yourself, make a suggestion, tell me about something you've learnt, whatever you want.
